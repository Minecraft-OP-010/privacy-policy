Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains that our Discord bot does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

Information We Do Not Collect

Our bot does not collect or store any data, including but not limited to user IDs, message content, or profile information. The bot simply processes commands in real-time to fulfill user requests without retaining any information.

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any data, no user information is stored or processed beyond the temporary interaction with the bot for command execution.

Data Retention

No data is retained or stored. All interactions are handled in real-time and discarded immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Please review this page for the latest information.
