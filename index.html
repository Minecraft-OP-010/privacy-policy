Death Bot Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for Death Bot, also known as Death Sniper. We take your privacy seriously, and this page explains how our bot operates in a way that ensures your privacy is respected.

Information We Do Not Collect

Death Bot does not collect or store any personal data from users. We do not retain information such as:

  • User IDs
  • Message content
  • Discord profile data
  • Server activity

Our bot simply processes commands in real-time to provide functionality such as encoding, decoding, and user lookups without saving any data beyond the command’s immediate execution.

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any data, there is no long-term use of any information. All data is processed temporarily for command fulfillment and discarded immediately after the action is performed.

Third-Party Services

Death Bot does not share or transmit any data to third-party services, as no data is collected or stored.

Data Retention

There is no data retention. Once a command is processed, any temporary data is discarded immediately.

User Control

As a user, you have complete control over your interaction with Death Bot. Since we do not collect or store data, you do not need to worry about data removal or opt-out requests.

Changes to This Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in bot functionality. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage users to check back regularly for updates.
