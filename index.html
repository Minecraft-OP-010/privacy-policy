Death Bot Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for Death Bot, also known as Death Sniper. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how our bot operates to ensure your privacy is protected.

Information We Collect

Death Bot is designed for minimal data interaction. We do not collect or store any personal data from users. This includes:

  • User IDs
  • Message content
  • Discord profile data
  • Server activity

Data Usage

Death Bot processes commands in real-time to provide its functionality (encoding, decoding, user lookups). This processing is temporary, and no data is stored beyond the immediate execution of a command.

Third-Party Services

Death Bot does not share or transmit any data to third-party services.

Data Retention

We have no data retention policy because we do not store any user data. Once a command is processed, any temporary data used for that command is immediately discarded.

Your Control

As a user, you have complete control over your interaction with Death Bot. You do not need to worry about data removal or opt-out requests since no data is collected or stored.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in bot functionality. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage users to check back periodically for updates.
